Dream your dreams with open eyes and make them come true

Jainam Banquet Hall is the perfect choice for hosting special events in Bhandup, Mumbai. Our luxurious and spacious banquet halls, Jainam Classic Hall and Jainam Status Hall, can accommodate up to 500 and 1000 guests respectively, while our newly renovated Party Hall can host up to 200 guests for small gatherings.

Our banquet halls feature high ceilings, separate areas for each important part of your event, and a separate area for wedding ceremonies. The interiors are exquisitely decorated, giving each space a comfortable and airy atmosphere.

We specialize in hosting a variety of events, including wedding receptions, business functions, conventions, reunions, celebrations, fine dining, bridal showers, company luncheons, and more. Our full-service luxury banquet facility is perfect for social receptions, corporate functions, proms, and other special events.

Located in the heart of L.B.S.Marg, Bhandup-(W), our banquet hall is just minutes away from Bhandup station and conveniently adjacent to major banks and malls in Bhandup, making holding your next event here hassle-free. Choose Jainam Banquet Hall for your next event and experience luxury and elegance at its finest.
